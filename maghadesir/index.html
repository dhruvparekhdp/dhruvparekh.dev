INDIAN POLITICAL SYSTEM

Overview :

This course is designed to equip the students with basic concept of Indian Political System and its practical applications.
The main learning objectives of this course are as follows:

  • To make the students understand the structure, functions and constitutional provisions of Indian Political System.
  • To discuss with them the reasons and need of evolution of such political system in India.
  • To make them aware about Legislative, Executive and Judicial system and its functioning.
  • To teach them process of formation and functioning of Union Executive and Local self government.

About Dr. Ravi R. Shukla

Ravi Rameshchandra Shukla (Ph.D) has been teaching Political Science at R.D. National College, affiliated to University of Mumbai since 2009 and International Relations and Foreign Policy of India to PG course students at Mumbai Univesity. He was member of Maharashtra State Hindi Sahitya Academy, Ministry of Cultural Affairs, Maharashtra (2018-2020). He is honorary advisor to NETZACH’s research and development, a Mumbai based policy think tank. He has written more than 15 research papers on Indian Politics and International relations. He has authored books entitled, “Identity Politics of Dalits and BSP in UP” and Edited Indian Political System. He has done his Ph.D. from University of Mumbai. Besides that, he has MA (Politics), LLB (Criminology and Constitution), UGC-NET, MA (Criminology and Police Administration), PGdip in Journalism, PGdip in Human Rights . International Relationship, Diplomacy, Electoral Study, Human Rights and Identity Politics are the areas of his academic pursuits. Twice he has been awarded Associate fellowship by UGC-IUC, Indian Institute of Advanced Study, Rashtrpati Niwas, Shimla an MHRD institution (2012-14, 2018-20). He has successfully implemented two Minor Research Projects of UGC and Mumbai University. He has delivered several invited lectures on International relations and Indian Politics. He has also presented more than 30 papers in National and International Seminars and Conferences. He writes poems as his hobby.
